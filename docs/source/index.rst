Research IT on Readthedocs
===================================

**Research IT** manages and supports high performance computing (HPC) and large scale storage (LSS) services for researchers at Iowa State University.  Almost every form of research requires powerful computers and lots of storage.  We are focused on helping researchers find the best solutions for their particular IT needs.

Check out the :doc:`usage` section for further information, including
how to :ref:`installation` the project.

.. note::

   This project is under active development.
